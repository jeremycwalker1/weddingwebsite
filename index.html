We're Getting Married!

August 1, 2026 • Pony, MT

Where we met

Jeremy met Carly at a friend's house party, in September, 2018. She was visiting a friend and had not decided to move to Bozeman yet, but Jeremy made sure to make an impression. Carly moved to Bozeman later that fall, and Jeremy was quick to ask her on a date once she was settled. He took a little longer than most to make a move, giving her a high-five on the fifth date. But after 6 wonderful years of dating, they are so in love and excited to tie the knot.
